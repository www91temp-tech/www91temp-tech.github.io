循環與氣體交換

Q1 An open circulatory system __________.（開放式循環系統的特徵） ▾
A.is less common in crabs and beetles than in mice and snakes
B.unlike a closed circulatory system, does not rely on muscle contraction
C.allows interstitial fluid to mix freely with vascular fluid
D.restricts the backflow of blood by valves in the trachea
E.has no valves
正確答案
C — allows interstitial fluid to mix freely with vascular fluid
解析
開放式循環系統中，血管液（hemolymph）同時充當間質液，兩者可自由混合。見於節肢動物（甲殼類、昆蟲）和大多數軟體動物。
Q2 In a fish, blood circulates through __________, whereas in a mammal, it circulates through __________.（魚類 vs. 哺乳類血液循環迴路數） ▾
A.two circuits; four circuits
B.one circuit; two circuits
C.four circuits; two circuits
D.one circuit; four circuits
E.two circuits; one circuit
正確答案
B — one circuit; two circuits
解析
魚類心臟為單心房＋單心室，血液流經單一迴路。哺乳類（與鳥類）具雙循環：肺循環（pulmonary）與體循環（systemic）。
Q3 All known birds and mammals have __________.（鳥類與哺乳類循環系統的共同特徵） ▾
A.a double circulatory system and a four-chambered heart
B.a single circulatory system and a four-chambered heart
C.a double circulatory system and a three-chambered heart
D.a double circulatory system and a two-chambered heart
E.a single circulatory system and a two-chambered heart
正確答案
A — a double circulatory system and a four-chambered heart
解析
四腔心臟（左右心房＋左右心室）將含氧血與去氧血完全分離，形成肺循環與體循環雙迴路，效率最高。
Q4 What is unique about blood in pulmonary arteries compared with blood in other arteries?（肺動脈血液的特殊性） ▾
A.Blood in pulmonary arteries is always blue; it is red in all other arteries.
B.It is moving away from the heart.
C.It is moving toward the heart.
D.It is loaded with carbon dioxide.
E.There is no difference; it is the same as blood in other arteries.
正確答案
D — It is loaded with carbon dioxide.
解析
動脈（artery）定義為「離開心臟的血管」，與含氧量無關。肺動脈是唯一攜帶去氧血（高CO₂）的動脈。
Q5 Which chamber(s) of a human heart receive(s) and pump(s) oxygenated blood?（接收並泵出含氧血的心臟腔室） ▾
A.Left ventricle and left atrium
B.Left atrium and right atrium
C.Left atrium only
D.Right atrium and right ventricle
E.Left ventricle only
正確答案
A — Left ventricle and left atrium
解析
肺靜脈將含氧血送入左心房，再流至左心室泵入主動脈供全身使用。左側心臟負責體循環。
Q6 Pressure is greatest in the ventricles when __________.（心室壓力最大的時機） ▾
A.blood arrives at the right ventricle
B.blood arrives at the left atrium
C.AV valves are open and semilunar valves are closed
D.the ventricles contract
E.the atria contract during early systole
正確答案
D — the ventricles contract
解析
心室收縮（systole）時，強大肌肉壓力關閉 AV 瓣並推開半月瓣，此時心室壓力達到峰值。
Q7 The SA node generates an electrical impulse from its location in __________.（SA 節的位置與功能） ▾
A.the left atrium, causing ventricular contraction
B.the left ventricle wall
C.the right atrium, causing atrial contraction
D.the left ventricle, causing atrial contraction
E.none of the listed locations
正確答案
C — the right atrium, causing atrial contraction
解析
竇房結（SA node）位於右心房壁，是心臟的「節律器」，其電訊號先觸發心房收縮，再經 AV 節傳至心室。
Q8 A recording shows atria contracting normally, but every few beats the ventricles fail to contract. Which is probably malfunctioning?（心室偶爾不跳的可能原因） ▾
A.AV node
B.Aortic semilunar valve
C.Coronary artery
D.SA node
E.AV valve
正確答案
A — AV node
解析
房室結（AV node）負責將心房的電訊號延遲後傳至心室。若 AV 節功能異常，心室收縮頻率降低，稱為「heart block」。
Q9 Your blood pressure is 120/70. The "120" indicates __________, and the "70" indicates __________.（血壓數值的意義） ▾
A.pressure in the left ventricle; pressure in the right ventricle
B.arterial pressure; venous pressure
C.arterial pressure during heart contraction; arterial pressure during heart relaxation
D.pressure in the left ventricle; pressure in the left atrium
E.pressure in the systemic system; pressure in the pulmonary system
正確答案
C — 收縮壓 / 舒張壓（動脈壓）
解析
120 mmHg 為收縮壓（systolic，心室收縮時）；70 mmHg 為舒張壓（diastolic，心臟舒張時）。兩者均為動脈壓。
Q10 When we compare arteries and veins: walls of arteries are __________, blood pressure inside is __________, and blood is moving __________ the heart.（動脈 vs. 靜脈比較） ▾
A.thicker; higher; away from
B.thinner; lower; away from
C.thinner; lower; toward
D.thicker; lower; toward
E.thinner; higher; away from
正確答案
A — thicker; higher; away from
解析
動脈離開心臟，承受較高血壓，壁較厚有彈性。靜脈回流心臟，壓力低，壁薄並具瓣膜防止逆流。
Q11 What would you expect in an individual with low levels of blood protein in the capillaries?（血漿蛋白不足的後果） ▾
A.Increased movement of fluid into the capillaries from the extracellular environment
B.Increased blood pressure
C.A net gain of fluid in the capillaries
D.Decreased lymph
E.Edema in the body tissues
正確答案
E — Edema in the body tissues
解析
血漿蛋白（如白蛋白）產生膠體滲透壓，吸引組織液回到微血管。蛋白質不足 → 滲透壓降低 → 液體積聚於組織 → 水腫（edema）。
Q12 What is atherosclerosis?（動脈粥樣硬化的定義） ▾
A.Chest pain due to a partial blockage of the coronary arteries
B.Hardening of the arteries by accumulation of fatty deposits
C.Damage to or death of cardiac muscle tissue due to a blockage in the coronary arteries
D.A failure of blood to clot
E.Lack of oxygen to the brain, leading to a death of nervous tissue there
正確答案
B — Hardening of the arteries by accumulation of fatty deposits
解析
動脈粥樣硬化（atherosclerosis）= 脂肪斑塊（plaque）沉積在動脈壁，使血管硬化狹窄，是心肌梗塞與中風的主要原因。

Q1Why do freshwater fish excrete a large amount of very dilute urine?（淡水魚大量排稀尿的原因）▾
A.Because they live in a hypoosmotic solution, their cells take up excess water that must be excreted.
B.Because they live in a hypoosmotic solution, their cells tend to accumulate a lot of solute.
C.Because they live in a hyperosmotic solution, their cells take up excess water.
D.Because they live in a hyperosmotic solution, their cells accumulate a lot of solute.
E.They excrete solutes to make concentration equal.
正確答案
A — 淡水（低滲）環境中水不斷滲入，需大量排尿
解析
淡水魚體液比環境高滲（hyperosmotic to environment），水由外環境經滲透持續流入，必須以大量稀釋尿液排出多餘水分。
Q2Most aquatic animals excrete ammonia, whereas land animals excrete urea or uric acid. What is the most likely explanation?（水生 vs. 陸生動物含氮廢物差異）▾
A.They have different diets.
B.Land animals can get the energy needed to make urea or uric acid.
C.Ammonia is very toxic, and it takes a lot of water to dilute it.
D.Land animals cannot afford the energy needed to make ammonia.
E.Fish need to get rid of ammonia, but land animals need it to live.
正確答案
C — 氨高毒性需大量水稀釋，陸生動物水資源有限故轉化為尿素/尿酸
解析
氨（ammonia）毒性強，需大量水稀釋後才能安全排出。水生動物水源充足可直接排氨；陸生動物則將氨轉化為毒性較低的尿素（需少量水）或尿酸（幾乎不需水）。
Q3The most effective molecule for nitrogenous waste disposal in desert animals would be __________.（沙漠動物最佳含氮廢物）▾
A.ammonia because it uses less energy to make
B.urea because it is less toxic than uric acid
C.uric acid because it takes less energy to make than urea
D.uric acid because it requires very little water for excretion
E.ammonia because it is the most soluble
正確答案
D — 尿酸（uric acid）可以晶狀固體形式排出，耗水量極少
解析
尿酸不溶於水，可形成半固體糊狀排出，對水分保存極為有利。這是沙漠爬蟲類、鳥類的重要適應。
Q4Which excretory system contains structures called flame bulbs that function in filtration?（含有焰球細胞的排泄系統）▾
A.Metanephridia
B.Protonephridia
C.Malpighian tubules
D.Kidneys
E.Nephrons
正確答案
B — Protonephridia（原腎管）
解析
原腎管見於扁蟲等無脊椎動物，含纖毛焰球細胞（flame bulb cells），纖毛擺動過濾體液。後腎管（metanephridia）見於環節動物；馬氏管（Malpighian tubules）見於昆蟲。
Q5The filtrate is first refined and concentrated by the processes of __________, which form the urine that leaves the body.（尿液形成的主要過程）▾
A.filtration and secretion
B.reabsorption and secretion
C.reabsorption and excretion
D.filtration and reabsorption
E.secretion and excretion
正確答案
B — reabsorption and secretion
解析
濾液形成後經：①再吸收（reabsorption）：將有用物質（葡萄糖、胺基酸、水）送回血液；②分泌（secretion）：將多餘離子、藥物等從血液送入腎小管。兩者共同精製濾液成尿液。
Q6The bed of capillaries in a vertebrate kidney where water, urea, and salts are filtered out of the blood is the __________.（腎臟過濾血液的微血管叢）▾
A.Bowman's capsule
B.collecting duct
C.glomerulus
D.loop of Henle
E.proximal convoluted tubule
正確答案
C — glomerulus（腎小球）
解析
腎小球是高壓微血管叢，血壓強迫水、尿素、鹽類等小分子穿過血管壁進入鮑曼囊（Bowman's capsule），形成初始濾液。
Q7In each nephron, the glomerulus and Bowman's capsule __________.（腎小球 + 鮑曼囊的功能）▾
A.filter the blood and capture the filtrate
B.reabsorb water into the blood
C.break down harmful toxins and poisons
D.reabsorb salts and nutrients
E.refine and concentrate the urine for excretion
正確答案
A — filter the blood and capture the filtrate
解析
鮑曼囊包圍腎小球，利用靜水壓（hydrostatic pressure）使血漿成分濾入囊腔，收集成濾液。大分子（蛋白質、血球）不能通過。
Q8As filtrate passes through the long loop of Henle, salt is removed, concentrating the interstitial fluid. Because of this high salt concentration, the nephron is able to __________.（亨氏環的作用結果）▾
A.excrete the maximum amount of salt
B.neutralize toxins that might accumulate in the kidney
C.control the pH of the interstitial fluid
D.excrete a large amount of water
E.establish a hyperosmotic interstitial medullary concentration
正確答案
E — establish a hyperosmotic interstitial medullary concentration（建立腎髓質高滲濃度梯度）
解析
亨氏環透過逆流增益（countercurrent multiplication）機制，在腎髓質間質建立高滲梯度，使集尿管中的水可經滲透作用被再吸收，最終產生濃縮尿液。
Q9What is the function of the ascending loop of Henle?（亨氏環升支的功能）▾
A.It provides water for reabsorption by the interstitial fluid and capillaries.
B.It loses urea to the renal medulla.
C.It absorbs some drugs and poisons from surrounding capillaries.
D.It helps maintain the concentration gradient of NaCl in the interstitial fluid, thus increasing water reabsorption.
E.It collects processed filtrate from the nephrons.
正確答案
D — 維持間質 NaCl 濃度梯度，促進水再吸收
解析
升支管壁對水不通透，NaCl 在內髓以擴散、外髓以主動運輸方式送至間質，維持高滲梯度，使降支與集尿管的水能持續被動滲出。
Q10What is the function of antidiuretic hormone (ADH) in the body?（ADH 的功能）▾
A.During times of increased solute concentrations, ADH causes nephrons to absorb water faster.
B.During times of higher solute concentrations, ADH causes more water to be released from the nephrons to be reabsorbed by the blood.
C.Low levels of ADH are the brain's response to thirst.
D.ADH is the only hormone that provides control over the kidney as an osmoregulator.
E.ADH controls the rate that filtrate moves from the glomerulus into Bowman's capsule.
正確答案
B — ADH 提高集尿管通透性，促使水再吸收回血液
解析
血液滲透壓升高時（脫水），下視丘偵測並觸發腦垂體後葉釋放 ADH；ADH 作用於集尿管，插入水通道蛋白（aquaporin），使水大量再吸收，尿量減少、濃度上升。
Q11Secretion of ADH from the __________ occurs in response to __________ and causes __________.（ADH 分泌的完整機制）▾
A.thyroid gland; low blood sugar; kidney blood vessels to constrict
B.pituitary gland; low blood osmolarity; decreased permeability to water
C.adrenal gland; high blood osmolarity; increased permeability to water
D.pituitary gland; high blood osmolarity; increased permeability to water of a collecting duct
E.adrenal gland; low blood pressure; increased water and solute reabsorption at the proximal tubule
正確答案
D — 腦垂體 / 高血液滲透壓 / 集尿管通透性增加
解析
三個關鍵：① 來源：腦垂體後葉（posterior pituitary）；② 刺激：高血液滲透壓（脫水狀態）；③ 效果：集尿管水通透性增加 → 水再吸收 → 尿液濃縮。
Q12What conditions are responsible for the stimulation of the juxtaglomerular apparatus (JGA)?（JGA 被激活的條件）▾
A.An increase in blood pressure or blood volume within the heart
B.An increase in the solute concentration of the blood plasma
C.A decrease in the solute concentration of the blood plasma
D.A decrease in the blood pressure or blood volume in the afferent arteriole
E.Consumption of alcohol
正確答案
D — 入球小動脈血壓或血量降低
解析
JGA 偵測到血壓/血量下降 → 分泌腎素（renin）→ 活化血管收縮素 II（Angiotensin II）→ 血管收縮＋醛固酮分泌增加Na⁺再吸收 → 血壓回升（RAAS 系統）。

Q1Which of the following is not a form of asexual reproduction?（哪項不是無性生殖）▾
A.Budding
B.Fragmentation and regeneration
C.Parthenogenesis
D.Hermaphroditism
E.None of the listed responses is correct.
正確答案
D — Hermaphroditism（雌雄同體）
解析
雌雄同體個體同時具有雌雄生殖系統，但生殖方式仍為有性生殖（精卵結合），不屬於無性生殖。
Q2An ovarian follicle undergoes several changes. Which sequence is correct, beginning with the oocyte?（卵巢週期正確順序）▾
A.Corpus luteum development, follicle development, ovulation
B.Follicle development, ovulation, corpus luteum development
C.Corpus luteum development, ovulation, follicle development
D.Follicle development, corpus luteum development, ovulation
E.None of the listed responses is correct.
正確答案
B — 濾泡發育 → 排卵 → 黃體形成
解析
卵巢週期：① 濾泡期（follicular phase）：FSH 刺激濾泡發育；② 排卵（ovulation）：LH 峰值觸發；③ 黃體期（luteal phase）：空濾泡轉化為黃體（corpus luteum），分泌黃體素與雌激素。
Q3Where do developing sperm cells undergo meiosis?（精子在哪裡進行減數分裂）▾
A.In the epididymis
B.In the vas deferens
C.In the seminal vesicle
D.In the seminiferous tubules
E.In the prostate gland
正確答案
D — In the seminiferous tubules（睪丸曲細精管）
解析
曲細精管（seminiferous tubules）是精子生成（spermatogenesis）的場所；精子隨後移至副睪（epididymis）成熟儲存。
Q4The __________ is/are the site of testosterone production in the testis.（睪固酮的產生位置）▾
A.seminiferous tubules
B.epididymis
C.Leydig cells
D.seminal vesicle
E.prostate gland
正確答案
C — Leydig cells（萊氏細胞）
解析
萊氏細胞（間質細胞，Leydig cells）散布於曲細精管之間，受 LH 刺激分泌雄性素（androgens），主要為睪固酮（testosterone）。
Q5Sperm cells have several components; the function of the acrosome is to __________.（頂體的功能）▾
A.produce a base to neutralize the acidic environment
B.metabolize the sugars for energy
C.release enzymes that help the sperm penetrate the egg
D.propel the sperm as they swim
E.perform all of the listed tasks
正確答案
C — 釋放水解酶穿透卵子外層
解析
頂體（acrosome）位於精子頭部前端，是特化的溶酶體，含水解酶。接觸卵子時發生頂體反應（acrosomal reaction），酶溶解卵子外膜（zona pellucida），使精子穿入。
Q6From a single primary oocyte, oogenesis in a mammal will produce __________.（卵子生成的產物）▾
A.four ova
B.four spermatozoa
C.an egg and two or three polar bodies
D.a primary oocyte
E.four polar bodies
正確答案
C — 1個卵 + 2~3個極體
解析
卵子生成與精子生成不同：減數分裂為不均等細胞質分裂，大部分細胞質保留給卵子，小的極體（polar bodies）通常退化。一個初級卵母細胞最終產生1個卵＋2~3個極體。
Q7In spermatogenesis, each primary spermatocyte gives rise to __________ sperm.（每個初級精母細胞產生幾個精子）▾
A.one
B.two
C.four
D.eight
E.millions of
正確答案
C — four（四個精子）
解析
初級精母細胞（2n）→ 減數分裂 I → 2個次級精母細胞（n）→ 減數分裂 II → 4個精細胞（n）→ 精子。精子生成為均等分裂，產生4個功能性精子。
Q8Which statement about luteinizing hormone (LH) in the female reproductive system is correct?（LH 在女性生殖中的作用）▾
A.It is produced by the hypothalamus.
B.It causes mitosis of the primary oocyte.
C.It inhibits ovulation.
D.It triggers development of endometrium in the corpus luteum.
E.It promotes the release of the secondary oocyte from the ovary.
正確答案
E — LH 峰值觸發排卵
解析
雌激素升至峰值後產生正回饋，觸發腦垂體大量分泌 LH（LH surge）→ 卵巢濾泡破裂、排卵。LH 同時促使空濾泡轉化為黃體。
Q9After ovulation occurs, the empty follicle __________.（排卵後空濾泡的變化）▾
A.can be recycled to produce more eggs
B.changes into the corpus luteum and makes hormones
C.quickly degenerates
D.immediately initiates menstruation
E.becomes the site of the implantation of a fertilized egg
正確答案
B — 轉化為黃體，分泌黃體素與雌激素
解析
排卵後濾泡細胞在 LH 作用下轉化為黃體（corpus luteum），分泌黃體素（progesterone）與雌激素，維持子宮內膜。若未受孕，約2週後黃體退化，月經開始。
Q10After the menstrual period, which hormone is the first to increase significantly, initiating the ovarian cycle?（月經週期開始時最先升高的賀爾蒙）▾
A.Progesterone
B.Follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH)
C.Estrogen
D.Luteinizing hormone (LH)
E.Human chorionic gonadotropin (hCG)
正確答案
B — FSH（濾泡刺激素）最先升高
解析
黃體退化、雌黃體素降低 → 解除對腦垂體的抑制 → FSH 上升 → 刺激少數濾泡開始發育、分泌雌激素 → 啟動新週期。
Q11What is the source of the hormones that, when suddenly absent, are directly responsible for the onset of menstruation?（月經來潮的賀爾蒙機制）▾
A.Hypothalamus
B.Pituitary
C.Ovarian follicle
D.Corpus luteum
E.Vestibular glands
正確答案
D — Corpus luteum（黃體）退化導致月經
解析
黃體分泌雌激素和黃體素維持子宮內膜。若未受孕，黃體在約14天後退化 → 兩種荷爾蒙驟降 → 子宮內膜血管收縮脫落 → 月經。

E — 防衛高能量食源 = 最適覓食
解析
最適覓食理論（optimal foraging theory）：動物以最小能量消耗獲取最大淨能量收益。太陽鳥只在保衛高產蜜花時的能量收益大於消耗時才防衛，完美體現能量成本效益計算。
Q9Off in the distance, you spot four female deer and a single male deer. This reflects which type of mating system?（一雄多雌的交配制度名稱）▾
A.Polygyny
B.Polyandry
C.Monogamy
D.Monogyny
E.None of the listed responses can be applied to this mating system.
正確答案
A — Polygyny（多妻制：一雄多雌）
解析
交配制度：Polygyny（多妻）= 一雄多雌（鹿、獅子）；Polyandry（多夫）= 一雌多雄；Monogamy（單偶）= 一對一；Promiscuity（雜交）= 無固定配偶。
Q10Altruistic behavior __________.（利他行為的正確描述）▾
A.never occurs in natural populations
B.is expected when the species as a whole benefits from it
C.occurs only when closely related individuals benefit from it
D.is expected when it increases the long-term inclusive fitness of the altruists
E.is expected when there are plentiful resources to share
正確答案
D — 提升利他者的整體適應度（inclusive fitness）
解析
利他行為（altruism）在個體直接適應度降低的同時，若能透過幫助具有共同基因的親屬（kin selection）或互惠（reciprocal altruism）提升包含適應度（inclusive fitness = 直接 + 間接），即可被天擇保留。
Q11The enhanced reproduction of related individuals with common alleles through apparently altruistic behavior is referred to as __________.（透過利他行為幫助親屬傳遞基因的機制）▾
A.Hamilton's rule
B.kin selection
C.competitive exclusion
D.adaptive radiation
E.the coefficient of relatedness
正確答案
B — kin selection（親緣選擇）
解析
親緣選擇（kin selection）：幫助具有共同等位基因的親屬繁殖，可間接增加自身基因在下一代的頻率。Hamilton's rule：rB > C（r=親緣係數，B=受益者獲益，C=施惠者代價）。工蜂犧牲繁殖機會照顧姐妹蜂后為典型例子。
Q12Which of the following well-known sayings best summarizes the idea of reciprocal altruism?（最能總結互惠利他的諺語）▾
A.A rolling stone gathers no moss.
B.You scratch my back, and I'll scratch yours.
C.A penny saved is a penny earned.
D.A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush.
E.Beauty is in the eye of the beholder.
正確答案
B — "You scratch my back, and I'll scratch yours."
解析
互惠利他（reciprocal altruism）：幫助非親緣個體，期待對方日後回報。條件：個體能再次相遇、能辨識對方、作弊者會受到懲罰。吸血蝙蝠分享血食即為著名例子。
